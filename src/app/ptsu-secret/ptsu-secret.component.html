You've probably figured out by this point that PTSU Exposed is not sending any psychoelectric mind waves from your phone. However, you probably haven't figured out how your friend is able to guess the brand that you enter out of the list every time, seemingly like magic. You may have thought to yourself, "...maybe the PTSU is real...?"

PTSU Exposed is nothing more than a magic trick, but it is kind of special. Most magic tricks are only performed once, because repeating them may reveal the trick or make it uninteresting. PTSU Exposed is different, because it can be performed over and over, and there is no chance that the victim will figure out how you are able to guess the brand every time without a hitch. They can enter any brand they want, and you'll always be able to guess it. It's a great way to make your friends and family hate you!

Before I get into the trick, I'm going to run through how PTSU Exposed should be performed. If you like puzzles, you might want to try and figure out the trick for yourself. It's difficult, but not impossible. Here's a hint: ask yourself, "what distinguishes the user-entered brand from all of the other brands in the list?" You can read the How to Start section; it contains no spoilers regarding the trick.

How to Start (No trick spoilers)

  1. Open www.tannerkrewson.com/ptsu on your phone.
  2. Sit you victim down, and hand them your phone. Tell them to read the prompts in the intro out loud.
  3. Eventually, they will be prompted to enter a brand to send to your brain. Make sure that they know you haven't seen them enter it.
  4. The phone will "send" the brand to your head through "brain waves".
  5. They will soon being reading the list of eight brands. One of them will be the one they entered, and the others will be random.
  6. To their amazement, you will be able to guess the brand! I like to play it up. If the brand was "Doritos", I would say something like, "Hmm... I'm getting kind of hungry. I want to get my fingers covered in salty and cheesy dust goodness... Doritos." This strategy is great for increasing the anger levels.

Some notes: (No trick spoilers)

  • They'll probably want to try again, or have someone else enter a brand. Let them do whatever they want! As long as they follow the instructions on the screen, you'll always be able to guess it, and there's almost no chance they'll figure out the trick!
  • After awhile, you might want to tell them the trick so they don't hate you forever. Or, if you're mean, you tell only one person and have them perform it to the rest of the group. So many devious possibilities.
  • If you keep letting them try over and over, they might start to get tricky with you by reading brands that are not in the list. If I notice this, I will say, "Hmm... I didn't feel a tingle with that one. Did you read the ones on the list?" or "I think the brain waves might have missed my head."

The Trick (Spoilers, obviously)

As your victim reads the words, imagine the first letter of each brand in uppercase. The first letter of the brand they typed in will have at least one curved line, and the other seven brands' first letters will have only straight lines, OR vice versa. If you're confused, here's an example.

Here's a few brands: Skittles, Peeps, Mentos, Oreos. The capitalized first letters of these words are S, P, M, and O, respectively. Notice that all of the letters contain at least one curve... except for M. That means that Mentos would be the correct answer!

Here's another example: Disney, Nesquik, Milky Way, Adidas. See if you can figure it out! Out of all of the capitalized first letters (D, N, M and A), the D is the only one with a curved line, so the answer is Disney! Notice that the letter D does have a straight line, but it counts as curved because their are two possibilities: letters with one or more curved lines, OR letters no curved lines/all straight lines.

This trick is based on the Shuffle Games magic tricks from Master of Illusion for the Nintendo DS.
